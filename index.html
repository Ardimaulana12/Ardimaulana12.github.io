What is the meaning of report text?

Report text is a report in the form of text which presents information about something that is or is actually the result of analysis or systematic research. This text has one of the types of writing in English.

Report

A report is a specific form of writing that is organized around concisely identifying and examining issues, events, or findings. A report can be academic, technical, or even business related. Reports are written to deliver facts about a project, process, or a situation. When writing a report, we start with the cover which informs the title of the report, followed by other components. Here are components of a report.

Title : The name of a report

Content page : List of the contents of the report

Acknowledgement : Statement of an author's gratitude to other people

Abstract : A short summary of a report

Introduction : Setting out the aims and objectives, terms and definition

Background : All the background research

Methodology : Set of the chosen methodology and research methods

Findings/Analysis : Set of your main findings

Conclusions : Sum up the findings

Recommendations : List of recommendation from the funder

Executive summary : Brief statements of the main points

References/Bibliography : Reference section

Glossary : A list of technical or special words

Appendix : A section giving extra information

Not every report applies all the components in their writing. A business related report title, acknowledgement, then showing the data related to the topic and ended with the fin conclusions. However more elaborate and advance reports like a science project or research likely use more of the components.

Example of Written Report

1. Aim

The purpose of this research is to find out the efficiency of adding a new menu in a restaurant. This is accomplished by analyzing the customers' responses towards the new product and the sales of the new product.

2. Methodology

The data of this research were collected using two methods, by observation and interviews. During the observation, the researcher observed how many customers ordered the new menu assigned by the restaurant owner. The observation was conducted in separate times during a week.

This was done to get wider variety of customers On the other hand, to get the reviews about the new the researcher conducted a simple interview to the customers who ordered the new menu. This was done the customers' opinion about the new menu.

3. Findings

After conducting the research, the researcher found that the new meme reserved a positive feedback from the customers. I was shown by the number of customers ordering the new menu when the research was conducted. The interview results also showed that the majority of the costumers liked the new menu.

4. Conclusion

In conclusion, the new menu offered by the restaurant is welcomed by most of its costumers which result in more costumers coming into the restaurant and increasing sales to the restaurant.

Example of Spoken Report

Let's start with the aim. The purpose of this research is to find out the efficiency of adding a new menu in a restaurant to the sales. I choose this research since my father has a restaurant and I want to help improve the restaurant.

Next, let me tell you about how the methodology. The data of this research were collected using two methods by observation and interviews. The observation was done to know how many customers ordered the new menu and the interview was done to get customers opinions on the new menu.
